# Estimation Log

Track estimated vs. actual hours for completed backlog items. Updated weekly during the Friday sprint retrospective.

---

## How to Use

1. Add `estimate: Xh` and `actual:` to every new backlog item's frontmatter.
2. Fill in `actual:` before archiving any completed item.
3. During the weekly retro, transfer completed items to this table.
4. After 8+ data points, calculate your **correction coefficient**: avg(actual ÷ estimate).
5. Multiply future estimates by this coefficient to get a calibrated projection.

> Most people run 1.3–1.7x. If your coefficient exceeds 2.0, your task scoping is the problem — you're estimating subtasks, not full items.
