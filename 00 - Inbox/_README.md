# Inbox

This is the **fleeting notes** layer of the Zettelkasten. Dump anything here — half-formed thoughts, quotes that struck you, questions, observations, links. Don't worry about structure.

## The Rule: Process Within 48 Hours

Fleeting notes are not storage. They are a temporary holding area. Every item here should be reviewed regularly and either:

1. **Promoted** → rewritten as an atomic permanent note in `Notes/` and linked to its sources, then deleted from here
2. **Absorbed** → folds into an existing note in `Notes/` as a connection or refinement, then deleted from here
3. **Discarded** → wasn't worth keeping after all; delete it

A crowded Inbox is a sign the processing habit has lapsed. An empty Inbox means the system is working.

## How to Write a Fleeting Note

No format required. Speed is the point. Examples:

> "The impediment to action advances action." — connect to the idea of using obstacles as fuel?

> Noticed I work better in 25-minute bursts than open-ended sessions — why? Is this about task anxiety or time perception?

> What's the difference between discipline and motivation as a mechanism? They feel different in practice.

## What a Permanent Note Looks Like (the goal)

See `50 - Notes/` for examples. The test: could you hand this note to someone with no context and have it convey a complete, useful idea?
